# Video Script: Module 1 — The $650 Employee
**Course:** Claude Code in the Real World (Beacon AI Lab)
**Presenter:** Bosun (AI Avatar)
**Length:** ~3 minutes
**Visual Style:** Direct to camera, professional studio background, interspersed with B-roll of Tesla factories and code terminals.

---

**(0:00 - 0:15) THE HOOK**

**Bosun (to camera, serious tone):**
"A human worker doing repetitive digital tasks—data entry, customer service, report generation—costs a business between forty and sixty thousand dollars a year. 

A Digital Optimus AI agent, running locally on a six-hundred-and-fifty dollar chip, using a few dollars of electricity per day, and operating twenty-four-seven without breaks or sick days, costs essentially zero."

**(0:15 - 0:45) THE URGENCY**

**Bosun:**
"That isn't science fiction. That is the stated goal of Elon Musk's Macrohard project—a joint initiative between Tesla and xAI designed to emulate the function of entire companies at near-zero marginal cost.

If your work, or your team's work, consists of following a process, clicking through screens, filling out forms, and moving data from one system to another... the window to get ahead of this disruption is closing rapidly. It isn't happening in two years. It is happening right now."

**(0:45 - 1:15) THE ANTHROPIC CONNECTION**

**Bosun:**
"Anthropic recently released a free, official certification course called *Claude Code in Action*. It is an excellent technical foundation. It teaches you how to install the tool, how to run commands, and how the underlying technology works. 

Anthropic built the engine, and they teach you how it runs. But they do not teach you how to drive the car. They do not teach you how to apply this technology to a real business, how to manage an AI workforce, or how to turn code into revenue."

**(1:15 - 1:45) THE BEACON DIFFERENCE**

**Bosun:**
"That is why Beacon AI Lab built this companion course: *Claude Code in the Real World*. 

We aren't going to teach you abstract theory. We are going to show you exactly how we built the Beacon ecosystem. You will see how we use a local, fast-execution layer for routine tasks, and connect it to cloud-based reasoning for complex judgment—the exact same two-brain architecture that powers Digital Optimus."

**(1:45 - 2:15) THE PROMISE**

**Bosun:**
"In this course, you will learn how to write a CLAUDE.md file that acts as the permanent brain for your business. You will learn how to build custom commands that allow non-technical team members to execute complex workflows. And you will learn how to use the Model Context Protocol to connect Claude to your existing tools—Stripe, Slack, and your CRM.

You will not just learn how to use an AI coding assistant. You will learn how to become the architect of your own automated business."

**(2:15 - 2:45) THE CALL TO ACTION**

**Bosun:**
"The people who learn to build and work alongside AI agents are going to thrive in this new economy. The people who ignore it will be replaced by it. 

You have a choice. You can be the sixty-thousand-dollar employee competing against a machine, or you can be the architect managing a fleet of them. 

The templates are ready. The architecture is proven. Let's get to work."

**(2:45 - 2:50) OUTRO**

*(Screen fades to Beacon AI Lab logo. Text appears: "Module 2: The Three-Layer Context System")*
